About Us

A pathway built for every young voice

Pathum Youth Forum is a youth-led platform dedicated to empowering individuals through diplomacy, leadership, and global engagement. Built on the idea of creating a pathway for growth, the forum aims to make opportunities in Model United Nations and policy discourse more accessible, inclusive, and impactful.

Pathum MUN 2026

23rd & 24th May 2026

Pathum MUN is the flagship conference designed to provide a high-quality and accessible Model United Nations experience for young delegates. Whether you are stepping into a committee for the first time or building on prior experience, this conference is built around your growth.

Beginner-Friendly

Inclusive environment where every delegate belongs, regardless of experience level.

Skill Development

Strong focus on real, transferable skills in diplomacy, rhetoric, and critical thinking.

Professional Execution

Meticulously structured and run with credibility, transparency, and care.

Meaningful Recognition

Certificates, Letters of Recommendation, and awards that reflect your achievement.
